Flash Sign Banner Privacy Policy Effective May 11, 2026

Flash Sign Banner does not collect your data.

Flash Sign Banner is an iOS-only utility from Big Pants Apps. Your banners are created and stored locally on your device. We do not run ads, analytics, accounts, or a backend service for the app.

Details

What the app does locally

Flash Sign Banner lets you create, save, style, and present full-screen banner messages. The text, style choices, presets, and countdown placeholders you create are stored locally on your iPhone or iPad.

If you use Siri or App Shortcuts, Apple handles Siri voice recognition and shortcut processing. Flash Sign Banner receives the banner text needed to create or show your sign. Big Pants Apps does not receive that text.

If you choose to contact us by email, we will receive the information you send in that message, such as your email address, device details, and support question. We use support emails only to respond to you and improve the app.
