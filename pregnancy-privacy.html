Pregnancy Growth Tracker Privacy Policy Effective May 16, 2026

Pregnancy Growth Tracker keeps your data on your device.

Pregnancy Growth Tracker is an iOS-only app from Big Pants Apps. Your due date, units, pregnancy timeline state, and journal entries are stored locally on your device. We do not run ads, analytics, tracking, accounts, third-party APIs, or a backend service for the app.

Summary

Privacy at a glance

No data collection

Big Pants Apps does not collect, sell, share, or store personal data from Pregnancy Growth Tracker.

No ads or analytics

The app does not include advertising SDKs, analytics tools, tracking SDKs, or third-party APIs.

Local pregnancy data

Your due date, week estimate, unit preference, and journal entries stay on your device. They do not sync to Big Pants Apps servers.

No account or backend

The app does not require an account, login, backend profile, or cloud storage to use its pregnancy timeline and journal features.

Details

What the app does locally

Pregnancy Growth Tracker lets you follow pregnancy week by week, view approximate growth information, browse a 40-week timeline, see planning prompts, and keep a private journal. Information you enter or save in the app is stored locally on your iPhone or iPad.

The app may create local links that open a search in your browser or maps app for nearby classes, providers, or planning resources. Big Pants Apps does not receive those searches, your location, your due date, or your journal entries.

If you choose to contact us by email, we will receive the information you send in that message, such as your email address, device details, app version, and support question. We use support emails only to respond to you and improve the app.

Medical

Health information boundary

Pregnancy Growth Tracker provides general educational information only. It is not medical advice, not a medical device, and not a substitute for a care team.

For medical questions, symptoms, test results, appointments, or urgent concerns, contact your clinician or care team directly.
