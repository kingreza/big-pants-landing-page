Quick guide

Using Flash Sign Banner

Create a banner

Open the app, create a new banner, type your message, choose your text and background colors, then save it for later or show it right away.

Present full screen

Tap a saved banner to show it full screen. Use large, short messages for the best readability across a room or crowd.

Choose a preset

Use Still for simple signs, Scroll for longer text, Flash for attention, Mirror for glass/window use, and the motion presets when you need extra visibility.

Use countdowns

Countdown placeholders can be written inside a message, such as Back in [2:00m]. The countdown updates while the banner is presented.

Keep it readable

Short words, high contrast, and fewer lines usually work best. If people need to read it quickly, make the message simpler.

Troubleshooting

Things to try first

The sign is hard to read

Try a shorter message, a larger font size, and a high-contrast pair like gold on black or black on white.

A countdown does not look right

Check the placeholder format and keep it simple, for example [2:00m]. If it still behaves unexpectedly, send us the exact text you entered.

You reached the saved banner limit

The free version is planned to support up to five saved banners. A one-time unlock is planned for unlimited saved banners.

Contact
