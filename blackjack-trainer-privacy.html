Blackjack Drill Trainer Privacy Policy Effective May 16, 2026

Blackjack Drill Trainer keeps practice data on your device.

Blackjack Drill Trainer is an iOS-only app from Big Pants Apps. Your table rules, practice progress, weak-hand history, stats, and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not run ads, analytics, tracking, accounts, third-party APIs, or a backend service for the app.

Summary

Privacy at a glance

No data collection

Big Pants Apps does not collect, sell, share, or store personal data from Blackjack Drill Trainer.

No ads or analytics

The app does not include advertising SDKs, analytics tools, tracking SDKs, or third-party APIs.

Table rules, attempts, accuracy, streaks, weak-hand history, stats, and preferences stay on your device.

No account or backend

The app does not require an account, login, backend profile, or cloud storage to use practice, reference, and progress features.

Details

What the app does locally

Blackjack Drill Trainer lets you practice blackjack basic strategy, tune table rules, review strategy charts, and track progress through local stats and heatmaps.

Information created while using the app is stored locally on your iPhone or iPad. It does not sync to Big Pants Apps servers, and Big Pants Apps does not receive your practice history, table settings, or progress data.

If purchases are added or unlocked in the app, they are handled by Apple through the App Store. Big Pants Apps does not receive your payment card details from Apple.

Training

Educational use only

Blackjack Drill Trainer is training software for learning blackjack basic strategy. It is not a gambling app, casino app, betting product, or financial tool.

The app does not include chips, wagers, bankroll management, casino affiliates, or real-money play. Practice decisions are for education only.
